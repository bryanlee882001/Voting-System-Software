\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classfinal_ranking}{final\+Ranking}} program implements functions that are related to determine the final ranking of the candidates/parties of the election as well as check for tie and flip a coin toss if necessary to determine the winner through an unbiased method.
